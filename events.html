Field Operations: Bequia 2026

St. Vincent and the Grenadines.


August 17 - Day 1

FAD Deployment

Construction of FAD and frame. Deployment at Trap Site #5 (Known good location). Underwater robot video test. 9-hour camera deployment.

August 18 - Day 2

Data Cycle

Camera retrieval, battery charging, SD card backup. Robot arm testing to release camera unit. Scuba divers on standby.

August 19 - Day 3

Compass Direction Study

Repeat deployment. Analyzing entry/exit compass directions of Lionfish to identify food sources.

August 20 - Day 4

Final Retrieval

Final camera run. Recovery of FADs via SCUBA team. Data compilation.
