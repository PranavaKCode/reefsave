The Challenge

Reefs are the ocean's nursery. Invasive Lionfish, with no natural predators in the Atlantic, are decimating juvenile fish populations. Traditional spearing is limited by SCUBA depths, and random trapping fails.

The Solution: 2025-2026 Project

Hypothesis: Location matters. Random deployment yields zero catch.

Method: We use FADs (Fish Aggregation Devices) combined with 9-hour video surveillance over 4 days to track "Jaw Zone", "Near Zone", and "Far Zone" activity. By correlating this with temp, depth, and tide data, we build a prediction model.
