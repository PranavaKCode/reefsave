The Mission

The mission of this non-profit is to research, educate, train and implement strategies to protect the reefs of the Western Atlantic and Caribbean Sea from loss of marine life from the Invasive Lionfish.

2025-2026 Agenda

  • Phase 1 (Done): Functionality of the Lionfish Purse Trap.
  • Phase 2 (Active): Location Prediction & FAD Aggregation Data.
  • Phase 3 (Planned): B-Corp Franchise Model.

Why Traditional Fishing Fails

Randomly deployed traps have a near-zero catch rate. Spearing is limited by SCUBA depth (130ft). Our strategy focuses on Location Prediction using Fish Aggregation Devices (FADs) to create predictable harvest zones.
