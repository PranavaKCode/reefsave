Who We Are

Mission: To research, educate, train and implement strategies to protect the reefs of the Western Atlantic and Caribbean Sea from loss of marine life from the Invasive Lionfish.


Strategy: Local people must receive economic benefit. We create self-funding systems where catching Lionfish creates wealth, allowing for political will and overcoming lack of resources.
