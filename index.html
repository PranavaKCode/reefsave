Saving Reefs.

Stopping Lionfish.

Researching, educating, and implementing strategies to protect the Western Atlantic & Caribbean from invasive predation.

Our Strategy View Roadmap

The Mission

Research

Developing a self-funding system to control Invasive Lionfish. We use data-driven testing to predict aggregation locations.

Economic Benefit

Local communities must benefit. We develope markets (Whole Foods) to ensure the catch creates wealth for local fishers.

Global Impact

Starting in the Caribbean (Bequia), our prediction models and trap designs are scalable to affected regions worldwide.

The Master Plan

Our strategy is broken down into three distinct phases to ensure sustainability and scalability.

  • Phase 1: Trap Functionality Testing (Completed)
  • Phase 2: Location Prediction (In Progress - Aug 2026)
  • Phase 3: B-Corp Franchise Model (Planned)
