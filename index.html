The Core Strategy

Research

Developing data-driven models to predict lionfish aggregation, moving beyond random trap placement to precise, effective harvesting.

Economics

Creating a self-funding system. Whole Foods has committed to buying the catch, creating wealth for local fishers.

Scalability

Starting in Bequia, expanding globally. Our Benefit Corporation franchise model ensures sustainable growth.
