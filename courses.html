Volunteer Training

Volunteers seeking to assist in field testing in August of 2026 are required to complete these courses.


Underwater Robot Operator

Lesson 1 - Operation and Control

+

The first lesson is to read and understand the operation and controls for the underwater robot. This lesson will walk you through each step of the process and provide a checklist to use for every deployment.

In addition to the Lesson Fee, to complete the course you must have three In-Field Operation Lessons using the Underwater Robot, each costing $75 US Dollars. An additional expenses for each trip will be covered by ReefSave.

If you complete the course and attend the entire week August 17, 2026 to August 21, 2026, you will be eligible for up to $800 in reimbursement.

Lesson 2 - Schedule Field Training

+

The In-Field Training will require the scheduling of the boat in South Florida. You would be required to pay a fee for each event to cover the cost of fuel ($75). For the FAD Aggregation tests in August of 2026, there can be a maximum of 2 Underwater Robot Operators.

The Data Manager

Required to be aboard the boat used for testing in August 2026 for the entire week.


Lesson 1 - Data Collection Procedures

+

Outlines the types of data to be collected and the manner in which each type of data is obtained. There are five total lessons that must be completed to qualify as Data Manager.

Lesson 2 - Participants & Locations

+

Good Location Prediction is the Goal. Includes forms, explanation of field names, and procedures used for data collection objectives.

Lesson 3 - Data Back Up

+

Data Back-Up is Mission Critical. It is the Responsibility of the Data Manager to confirm that all the resources and equipment needed is available.

Lesson 4 - Daily Logs

+

Multi-day Field Projects Require Clear Daily Logs. Capturing data in real time and recording impressions and notes is vital. This lesson includes forms and procedures.

Lesson 5 - Organization & Summary

+

Both During the Field Project and Afterwards, you are Responsible for the collection, Preservation, and Prevention of Loss of the Data. Protection of Data is critical to Raising Capital.
